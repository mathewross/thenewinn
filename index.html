About Us

The New Inn in Shrawley, a traditional country cosy pub located in the exceptionally scenic Malvern Hills District run by Gerry and Julie.

We are a dog friendly and family friendly pub, ideally located for walkers to come by after a hike in the local hills.
We pride ourselves on customer service and our great food, which is locally sourced. Take a look at our Menu, we will always do our best to meet any dietary needs.

The New Inn has kept its rustic charm, step in to meet the team and the locals. Your happiness is our goal, so feel free to check out previous reviews or even let us know what you think! Click here to find us on Facebook or click here to find us on Tripadvisor
